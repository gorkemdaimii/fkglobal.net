About Us

Welcome to F.K Global Furniture, where passion, precision, and the allure of Turkish craftsmanship converge. Founded by Fatih Çelik and Kaan Demirel, we are on a mission to bring the beauty of quality and modern Turkish furniture to homes worldwide.

MISSION

"At F.K Global Furniture, our mission is to seamlessly integrate the artistry of Turkish craftsmanship into homes worldwide. We are dedicated to curating a diverse collection of quality and modern furniture, produced with precision by our extensive network of manufacturers in Turkey. By upholding the values of transparency, trust, quality, accessibility, and sincerity, we aspire to consistently provide our customers with affordable, timeless pieces that enrich their living spaces."

VISION

"F.K Global Furniture envisions a future where our commitment to quality, modernity, and affordability propels us to become the world's foremost furniture exporter within the next five years. Guided by our core values of transparency, trust, quality, accessibility, and sincerity, we aim to redefine the global furniture landscape. We aspire to be the go-to destination for those seeking the finest Turkish craftsmanship, setting new benchmarks in the industry and making a lasting impact on homes around the world."

OUR FOUNDERS

Meet the visionaries behind F.K Global Furniture – Fatih Çelik and Kaan Demirel. Their shared commitment to transparency, trust, quality, accessibility, and sincerity forms the foundation of our company. Inspired by the rich heritage of Turkish craftsmanship, they established F.K Global Furniture with a vision to make Turkish design a global household name.

CRAFTING EXCELLENCE

At F.K Global Furniture, we take pride in our role as ambassadors of Turkish artistry. Our extensive network of manufacturers in Turkey allows us to offer a wide range of meticulously crafted, modern furniture. Each piece embodies the seamless blend of tradition and innovation that defines Turkish design.

OUR CORE VALUES

Trust, quality, accessibility, and sincerity are more than just words to us; they are the pillars upon which F.K Global Furniture stands. We believe in providing not just furniture but an experience grounded in these values. Your trust is our greatest asset, and we are dedicated to delivering products that meet your needs in terms of quality and affordability.

A GLOBAL VISION

Our vision is bold – within the next five years, we aspire to become Turkey's largest furniture exporter. With an unwavering commitment to our core values, we aim to redefine the global furniture market and set new standards. F.K Global Furniture is not just a brand; it's a promise to bring the elegance and charm of Turkish design to homes across the globe. Thank You for Choosing F.K Global Furniture As we embark on this journey, we extend our gratitude to you for choosing F.K Global Furniture. Join us in creating spaces filled with sophistication, comfort, and the timeless beauty of Turkish craftsmanship.
